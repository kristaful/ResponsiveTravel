The World's Largest Fork

Fork Etiquette

Welcome to the world's largest fork, standing 35 feet, made of polished stainless steel, and signifying the home of The Food Channel. Please enjoy it and be safe, but stay on the concrete. Hashtag your photos #worldslargestfork
